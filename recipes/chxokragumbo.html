Chichen and Okra Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Stewed Okra
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1 lb Shothered Chicken
  • 2 Cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 lb Celery
  • 2 lbs Diced Onions
  • 1 lb Diced Green Bell Pepper
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Cups Cooked Rice
  • Garnish Green Onion

Method

  1. Dice Onions, Bell Pepper, and Celery
  2. Melt Butter Sautee Onions for an hour or two
  3. Add Chicken Stock and Simmer for an hour
  4. Add Stewed Okra
  5. Add Smothered Chicken
  6. Put gumbo on rice or potato salad garnish with Green Onions
