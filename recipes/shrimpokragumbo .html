Chichen and Okra Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Stewed Okra
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • 1 lb Shrimp
  • 2 Cups Seafood Stock
  • 1 lb Celery
  • 3 lbs Diced Onions
  • 1 lb Diced Green Bell Pepper
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Cups Cooked Rice
  • Garnish Green Onion

Method

  1. Dice Onions, Bell Pepper, and Celery
  2. Melt Butter Sautee Onions for an hour or two
  3. Add Seafood Stock and Simmer for an hour
  4. Add Stewed Okra
  5. Add Shrimp
  6. Put gumbo on rice or potato salad garnish with Green Onions
